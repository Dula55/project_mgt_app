Troubleshooting Guide

  1. Enable 2-Factor Authentication on your Google account
  2. Generate an App Password:
    • Go to Google Account → Security
    • Under "Signing in to Google", select "App passwords"
    • Select "Mail" as the app and "Other" as the device
    • Copy the 16-character password
  3. Use the app password in your .env file

  • "Less secure apps" error: Gmail no longer supports less secure apps. Use App Password instead.
  • Authentication failed: Check your email and password in .env file
  • Connection timeout: Check your internet connection and firewall settings
  • Sender address rejected: MAIL_DEFAULT_SENDER must match MAIL_USERNAME
